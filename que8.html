The Way.
You share it between the two of you. The next morning, you continue walking, and you trip over a stone. The coin falls out of your pocket. Thinking that you already found the treasure and betrayed them, they tie you up on a nearby palm tree. You tell them that the injured man has the coins too. They take the injured man with them to where they came from to find the treasure.
The sun sets and rises, and there is no sign of them coming back.
